Synopsis:
The Great Gatsby is a 1925 novel set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, written by F. Scott Fitzgerald. It tells the story of the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his obsessive passion for Daisy Buchanan, exploring themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream.
