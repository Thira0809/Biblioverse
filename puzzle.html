Puzzles.

by Harper Russo

Synopsis:
Puzzles by Harper Russo is a contemporary mystery novel that intertwines logic, emotion, and hidden truths. When a young journalist uncovers a decades-old secret linked to a series of riddles, she must solve the puzzles to reveal the truth about her past and her family’s legacy.
